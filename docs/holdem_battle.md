# Hold'em Battle

Hold'em is source-level implemented as a first-pass playable battle mode. Unity Editor compile, scene generation, EditMode, play, and build validation are still required in an isolated validation worktree before treating it as Unity-validated.

This supersedes the earlier v0.1 assumption that Hold'em was placeholder-only and routed to dice.

## Routing

- `CharacterType.Holdem` routes to `HoldemBattleScene`.
- The scene is generated from `Tools/Build HoldemBattle Scene`.
- Do not hand-edit `Assets/Scenes/HoldemBattleScene.unity`; regenerate it from the builder.
- If stable runtime scenes are tracked, `Assets/Scenes/HoldemBattleScene.unity` and `Assets/Scenes/HoldemBattleScene.unity.meta` must be generated by Unity and reviewed as generated artifacts before commit.
- If the scene remains generated-only, run `Tools/Build HoldemBattle Scene` before play/build validation so the scene exists and is appended to `EditorBuildSettings`.

## Player Attack Round

- The player has 2 face-up hole cards.
- Five community cards are generated face-down for the first player attack turn.
- There is no manual community-card reveal button.
- Community reveal advances automatically when the next player attack turn starts after a confirmed player attack and enemy counterattack:
  - Stage 1: initial turn, 1 community card visible, 3 total visible cards.
  - Stage 2: after one player attack turn has passed, 3 community cards visible, 5 total visible cards.
  - Stage 3: after two player attack turns have passed, 5 community cards visible, 7 total visible cards.
- If the player attacks while all 5 community cards are visible and combat continues, the next player attack turn keeps the 2 hole cards and deals a new set of 5 community cards immediately face-up.
- The player can attack at any reveal stage.
- Enemy counterattack starts only after attack confirmation.

## Multipliers And Damage

- Stage multipliers default to `2.4 / 1.6 / 1.0`.
- Optional debug constants remain available as `3.0 / 2.0 / 1.0`.
- High Card attacks the selected target only.
- One Pair or better deals full AOE damage to every living enemy.
- Any single attack is capped at 80 damage after multiplier.

## Redraws

- Hole card 1 can be redrawn exactly 2 times per player attack round.
- Hole card 2 can be redrawn exactly 2 times per player attack round.
- Community cards can be fully redrawn once per player attack round.
- Community redraw replaces all current community cards, while preserving both hole cards and current reveal count.
- Redrawn-away cards remain excluded from the same player attack round. A new full-community cycle uses a fresh deck excluding the current hole cards.

## Defense

- Living enemies counterattack one by one after the player's attack.
- Each enemy shows one attack card.
- The player chooses one of 5 face-down defense cards.
- If the chosen defense rank is greater than or equal to the enemy card rank, the attack is blocked.
- Otherwise the enemy damage applies through the existing player heart pipeline.

## Visual Composition

This scene is being treated as a visual-first card-battle UI pass before adding more Hold'em mechanics.

- Enemy zone stays in the upper half with the existing battle enemy slots.
- The shared table row uses 5 centered community-card slots in the lower-middle play mat, above the player's hand.
- The player's 2 hole cards sit at the bottom as angled held cards, visually closer than the community row.
- During defense, the acting enemy gets a floating attack card near its body, with a matching reference card in the defense panel.
- Defense prompts are shown through an in-scene battle message banner, not only the scrolling battle log.
- The defense selection row shows 5 face-down selectable card objects with hover, lift, highlight, flip, and result feedback.
- Fallback card faces, backs, shadows, borders, and mat panels must remain presentable when generated/imported PNG art is absent.

## Asset Provenance

Hold'em card backs, blank fronts, and suit pips should be generated or imported from generated outputs, then assembled deterministically into usable front-card sprites. Do not treat those image assets as hand-authored art. See `docs/holdem_image_prompts.md`.

The current card-art import folder is `Assets/Holdem/Sprites/Cards/`, with full front-card sprites under `Assets/Holdem/Sprites/Cards/Fronts/`. No Hold'em PNG assets are required for the builder to compile at source level. When the PNG files are absent, `HoldemBattleSceneBuilder` uses colored card and mat fallbacks. Do not commit generated image assets without their Unity `.meta` files.
